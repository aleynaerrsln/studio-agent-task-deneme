# Heads Up — Upadacitinib vs Dupilumab Klinik Çalışması

> **Kaynak:** JAMA Dermatology 2021;157(9):1047-1055
> **Çalışma:** Heads Up (NCT03738397) — head-to-head, phase 3b, randomized, double-blinded
> **Yazarlar:** Blauvelt A, Teixeira HD, Simpson EL, et al.
> **Yayın:** Published online August 4, 2021. doi:10.1001/jamadermatol.2021.3023
> **Hastalık:** Moderate-to-Severe Atopic Dermatitis (AD)

---

## 1. GENEL BİLGİLER

### Hastalık
- **Condition:** Moderate-to-Severe Atopic Dermatitis (AD)
- **Target population:** Adults with atopic dermatitis symptoms for ≥3 years and Eczema Area and Severity Index (EASI) ≥16

### Örneklem
- **n = 673** (randomized ve treated)
- **n = 924** (assessed for eligibility, 251 excluded)
- **Gender distribution (total):** 375 Men, 298 Women
- **Age range:** 18-76 years (mean 36.3 y, SD 14.1)

### Eligibility
- Adults aged 18 to 75 years
- AD symptoms for ≥3 years
- EASI ≥16, vIGA-AD ≥3
- Weekly Worst Pruritus NRS ≥4 at baseline
- Prior JAK inhibitor or dupilumab use prohibited

### Çalışma Tasarımı
- **Tip:** Randomized clinical trial (RCT), head-to-head
- **Faz:** Phase 3b
- **Körleme:** Double-blinded, double-dummy
- **Süre:** 24-hafta (primary endpoint week 16)
- **Lokasyon:** 129 merkez, 22 ülke (Europe, North/South America, Oceania, Asia-Pacific)
- **Tarih:** February 21, 2019 — December 9, 2020

### Tedavi Kolları

| Grup | İlaç | Dozaj | n |
|---|---|---|---|
| Intervention | Upadacitinib | 30 mg oral once daily | 342 |
| Comparison | Dupilumab | 300 mg subcutaneous every 2 weeks (600mg loading) | 331 |

### Dağılım Bilgisi
- 1:1 randomization
- Efficacy analyses conducted in intent-to-treat population

## 3. PRIMARY ENDPOINT — EASI75 at Week 16

### Referans Değer: Superiority of upadacitinib vs dupilumab

### Analiz Yöntemi
- NRI-C (Nonresponder Imputation incorporating Multiple Imputation)
- Hierarchical testing procedure, 2-sided 0.05 level

### Primary Outcome Results

| Endpoint | Dupilumab n (%) | Upadacitinib n (%) | Difference | p-value |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **EASI75 at Week 16** | **207 (62.6)** | **248 (72.4)** | **9.7** | **0.007** |

### Öne Çıkan Bulgular
- **EASI75 at week 16 primary endpoint** — Upadacitinib superior to dupilumab
- Upadacitinib %72.4 (248/342) vs Dupilumab %62.6 (207/331)
- **Adjusted difference: 9.7% (95% CI, 2.6%-16.7%); p = 0.007**
- Primary endpoint superiority MET

## 7. INFOGRAFİK / VISUAL ABSTRACT İÇİN ANAHTAR MESAJLAR

1. **Head-to-Head RCT:** İlk direct comparison — upadacitinib vs dupilumab in atopic dermatitis
2. **Büyük Örneklem:** 673 hasta, 129 merkez, 22 ülke
3. **Superior Efficacy:** EASI75 at week 16 — Upadacitinib 72.4% vs Dupilumab 62.6% (p=0.007)
4. **Rapid Onset:** Week 2'de EASI75 — 44.3% vs 18.2% (3x hızlı)
5. **Better Complete Clearance:** Week 16 EASI100 — 28.4% vs 7.9%
6. **Itch Relief Faster:** Week 1'de pruritus -32.0% vs -8.9% (p<0.001)
7. **Safety Trade-offs:** Upadacitinib'te daha çok acne, herpes zoster, CPK elevation; dupilumab'ta daha çok conjunctivitis
8. **Klinik Anlam:** Upadacitinib moderate-to-severe AD'de superior ve daha hızlı etki

## 9. ÇALIŞMA SONUCU (CONCLUSIONS)

> "During 16 weeks of treatment, upadacitinib demonstrated superior efficacy vs dupilumab in patients with moderate-to-severe AD, with no new safety signals."

### Ana Çıkarım
**Upadacitinib 30 mg oral once daily**, moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis'te:
- Superior efficacy (daha yüksek skin clearance + itch relief)
- More rapid onset (week 1-2'de etki)
- Consistent with known safety profile (no new signals)
- Dupilumab ile karşılaştırıldığında tercih edilebilir option

### Klinik Anlam
Moderate-to-severe AD tedavisinde **oral JAK1 inhibitor** (upadacitinib), **IL-4/IL-13 reseptör blokeri** (dupilumab) ile kıyaslandığında daha yüksek ve daha hızlı remission oranları sağlamaktadır.
