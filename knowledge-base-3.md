# ETC Model — Financial Incentives for Home Dialysis in End-Stage Kidney Disease

> **Kaynak:** JAMA Health Forum 2022;3(10):e223503
> **Çalışma:** End-Stage Renal Disease Treatment Choice (ETC) model — multiyear, mandatory-participation cluster randomized clinical trial
> **Yazarlar:** Ji Y, Einav L, Mahoney N, Finkelstein A
> **Yayın:** Published October 7, 2022. doi:10.1001/jamahealthforum.2022.3503
> **Hastalık:** End-Stage Kidney Disease (ESKD)
> **Trial ID:** ClinicalTrials.gov Identifier: NCT05005572

---

## 1. GENEL BİLGİLER

### Hastalık
- **Condition:** End-stage kidney disease (ESKD)
- **Target population:** Medicare beneficiaries with ESKD aged 66 years or older who initiated dialysis treatment

### Örneklem
- **n = 18 621** patients initiated dialysis during study period
- **n = 302 HRRs** (Hospital Referral Regions randomized — cluster unit)
- **6000 dialysis facilities**
- **12 394 managing clinicians**
- **Gender distribution:** 10 765 Men (57.9%), 7856 Women (42.1%)
- **Age:** Mean (SD) 74.8 (1.05) years

### Eligibility
- Medicare beneficiaries aged 66 years or older
- Initiated dialysis for ESKD in 2021 (new to dialysis — no prior 12 months)
- Exclusions: acute kidney injury only, younger than 18 years, recent kidney transplant, Medicare Advantage, dementia, skilled nursing facility residents

### Çalışma Tasarımı
- **Tip:** Cluster Randomized Clinical Trial (RCT)
- **Randomization unit:** Hospital Referral Regions (HRRs) — cluster, not patient
- **Participation:** Mandatory
- **Süre:** Multi-year program (Jan 1, 2021 — June 30, 2026); bu analiz Year 1 (2021)
- **Lokasyon:** Dialysis facilities in the US — 302 HRRs nationwide
- **Stratification:** Census regions (Northeast, South, Midwest, West)
- **Implementer:** US Center for Medicare & Medicaid Innovation (CMMI)

### Tedavi Kolları

| Grup | Intervention | n (HRRs) |
|---|---|---|
| Treatment | ESRD facilities and managing clinicians received financial incentives for home dialysis use | 91 |
| Control | No intervention | 211 |

### Intervention Details
- **HDPA** (Home Dialysis Payment Adjustment): +3% in 2021, +2% in 2022, +1% in 2023
- **PPA** (Performance Payment Adjustment): −5% to +4% range in year 1, expanding to −10% to +8% by program end

## 3. PRIMARY ENDPOINT — Percentage of Any Home Dialysis in First 90 Days

### Definition
**The primary outcome was the percentage of patients with ESKD who received any home dialysis during the first 90 days of treatment.**

### Primary Outcome Results

| Group | Mean (SD) % Any Home Dialysis | 95% CI | p-value |
|---|---|---|---|
| Control (211 HRRs) | 20.60 (7.77) | — | — |
| Treatment (91 HRRs) | 20.72 (+0.12 pp) | −1.42 to 1.65 | 0.89 |

### Key Statistics
- **Mean difference: 0.12 percentage points**
- **95% CI: −1.42 to 1.65 percentage points**
- **P = 0.89** (statistically non-significant)
- **Result: Null — no evidence of effect from financial incentives in Year 1**

### Öne Çıkan Bulgular
- Treatment ve control HRR'lar arasında home dialysis oranında istatistiksel anlamlı fark yok
- 95% CI 1.65 puan üstü etkiyi dışlıyor
- Küçük incentive magnitude (3% HDPA + ±5% PPA) behavior change'e yeterli olmamış olabilir

## 7. INFOGRAFİK / VISUAL ABSTRACT İÇİN ANAHTAR MESAJLAR

1. **Key Question:** How did the End Stage Renal Disease Treatment Choice model affect home dialysis rates?
2. **Key Finding:** The mean percentage of patients with any home dialysis in the first 90 days was not statistically significantly different in the treatment and control group
3. **Intervention Type:** Financial incentives (policy-level, not clinical)
4. **Randomization Unit:** Hospital Referral Regions (HRRs) — cluster design
5. **Population:** 18 621 Medicare beneficiaries aged 66 or older with ESKD
6. **Scope:** US nationwide, 302 HRRs, 6000 facilities
7. **Implication:** Year-1 incentive magnitude may be insufficient; higher incentives in later years to be evaluated

---

## 8. ÇALIŞMA SONUCU (CONCLUSIONS)

> "In this evaluation of the first year of a randomized clinical trial of the ETC model for Medicare beneficiaries with ESKD, HRRs randomly assigned to the model did not have statistically significantly different rates in home dialysis than control HRRs. This raises questions about the efficacy of the financial incentives in the model and suggests that higher incentives may be necessary to affect behavioral change by dialysis clinicians and facilities."

### Ana Çıkarım
- İlk yıl ETC incentive'ları home dialysis oranını anlamlı olarak artırmadı
- Cluster RCT tasarımı confounding'e karşı önceki observational çalışmalardan daha güçlü sonuç veriyor
- İleriki yıllarda artan incentive büyüklüğü behavior change için gerekli olabilir
